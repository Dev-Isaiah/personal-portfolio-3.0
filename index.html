About Me

My Story

The path into web development started in an unexpected place—gaming and game development. Through the online communities I was part of, I met people from all kinds of backgrounds. One of those connections introduced me to the role of a full-stack developer, and that single moment shifted the entire direction of my path.

I began experimenting with front-end development at an early age and quickly became fascinated with building and improving digital experiences. Over time, that curiosity grew into a commitment to becoming a full-stack engineer—and beyond.

What drives me most is solving complex problems by breaking them down into manageable pieces. I leverage AI to handle repetitive tasks and reminders so I can focus on creativity, direction, and crafting effective solutions. For me, technology isn’t just about efficiency—it’s about freeing people to focus on what matters most.

That’s why I’m passionate about helping small business owners and administrators cut through the noise of technical challenges. My goal is to give them back their time and energy, so they can put their focus where it truly belongs: on the unique work that makes them productive and successful.

To me, productivity isn’t measured only by how much gets done in a day—it’s about the quality and impact of the solution. And that’s the standard I hold myself to in every project I take on.

Beyond the Code

Outside of development and design, fitness plays a major role in my life. For me, it’s not just about lifting weights or running—it’s about form, discipline, consistency, and the deeper understanding of how the human body adapts and grows. The progress isn’t only physical; it’s also a mindset that carries into everything else I do.

I also enjoy exploring new experiences and creative outlets. Whether it’s discovering refreshing drinks, watching reels of online creators and fitness influencers, or spending summer days kayaking on the water, I’m always looking for ways to recharge and stay inspired. Photography is another passion of mine—I love capturing landscapes and the sky, freezing those moments that remind me of how much there is to explore beyond the screen.

Project 1

SC Wake SeaDek

SC Wake’s website was built as a dedicated e-commerce platform to streamline retail sales of SeaDek flooring. The company needed a solution that was fast, search-optimized, and capable of managing thousands of SKUs without sacrificing performance.

After transitioning from WordPress, the site delivered immediate results—online sales surged, and the team highlighted how much easier it became to process and fulfill orders. In addition, automated workflows were integrated to support efficient order fulfillment and reduce redundancy, creating a more streamlined process that boosted productivity across the business.

Project 2

Equestrian Club Website

This responsive concept website was designed to introduce visitors to the club’s offerings and encourage participation. It features a clean navigation menu, a welcoming introduction, dedicated sections highlighting riding lessons, and a photo gallery that captures the experience.

With clear calls-to-action, operating hours, service details, and interactive “learn more” buttons, the site balances information with engagement. The result is a polished demonstration project that showcases how thoughtful design can guide visitors while highlighting the club’s unique atmosphere.
